About

The SAKEC Competition Cell is dedicated to keeping students informed and ready for opportunities beyond the campus. Our primary mission is to create awareness about prestigious national and international competitions, hackathons, and challenges happening around the globe. While we do conduct our own events, our core focus is bridging the gap between students and external opportunities, ensuring every student knows where to showcase their talent. We curate lists of upcoming contests, provide guidance on how to participate, and offer the support needed to excel on these global platforms.

Objectives

  • circle Foster Competitiveness: To instill a healthy spirit of competition and innovation among students.
  • circle Skill Development: To provide technical training and mentorship.
  • circle Encourage Participation: To facilitate student participation in hackathons and contests.
  • circle Celebrate Innovation: To recognize and showcase groundbreaking projects.
